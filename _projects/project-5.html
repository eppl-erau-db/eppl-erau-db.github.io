Description

The OpenMutt is a fully printed open source platform for quadruped design and development. It is a completely modular design, allowing for payload design and development by multiple teams. It is currently under the development by the Engineering Physics Propulsion Lab, in conjunction with the Mechanical Engineering Department's senior design teams.
